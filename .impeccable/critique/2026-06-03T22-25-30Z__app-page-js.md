## Overall Impression

The trust and accessibility problems from the last critique are resolved: hero contrast is AA-solid, the newsletter form is fully stateful, the stats are discoverable, and the services section carries real editorial intent. The single most worthwhile next move is removing the stats side-stripe (it's the one thing a reviewer would point at and say "AI"). After that, the page is a confident, shippable brand surface.

## What's Working

1. **The services rework landed.** Left-anchored heading + stacked image rows with dividers reads editorial and premium; it's the clearest win over the previous card grid.
2. **The form now earns trust.** Inline validation, a real success state in silver-tree, and errors in burnt-sienna make the one interactive moment feel finished, and quietly introduces the chromatic palette with semantic meaning.
3. **Hero is decisive.** The brand-navy scrim fixes the contrast and gives the fold a committed mood; the CSS first-load choreography is tasteful and survives reduced-motion / no-JS.

## Priority Issues

- **[P2] Stats side-stripe border.** `border-l-2` + torea-bay on the active stat is the textbook AI tell and the only deterministic finding. **Fix:** signal the active stat without a thick colored side border, e.g. a full subtle card (1px full border + `bg-torea-bay-lightest` + torea-bay figure), a bottom-aligned indicator, or a leading filled dot. Keep the discoverability win, drop the stripe. *Command:* `/impeccable layout`

- **[P3] Recurring kickers across three sections.** 体験 / サービス / 実績 now share an accent-dot system, which reads more intentional, but it's still a section eyebrow on three blocks. Decide whether it's a deliberate brand device or trim it to one. *Command:* `/impeccable typeset`

- **[P3] Conventional section framing.** Features (centered icon triplet), stats, and CTA still use the symmetric centered-header rhythm. Not a defect, but if you want the page to feel unmistakably Acty rather than "premium-wellness-generic," one more section breaking the center axis would do it. *Command:* `/impeccable bolder`
